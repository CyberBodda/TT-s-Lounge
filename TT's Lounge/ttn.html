Welcome to TT’s Lounge, your go-to destination for authentic oriental cuisine.

  • Our mission is simple: to bring the rich flavors of the East to your table, creating a dining experience that feels like home.
  • At TT’s Lounge, we believe that food is more than just nourishment — it’s a way to connect people, share stories, and celebrate culture.
  • Our chefs use fresh ingredients and traditional recipes to prepare every dish, from sizzling kebabs to fragrant rice bowls and warm, comforting teas.

Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a late-night craving, TT’s Lounge offers a cozy atmosphere and a warm welcome to everyone.Come hungry, leave happy — and let us take you on a culinary journey through the flavors of the Orient.

Egyptian cuisine is a rich and diverse culinary tradition that blends Pharaonic, Middle Eastern, and Mediterranean influences. Known for its hearty, flavorful dishes, Egyptian food is a celebration of community, hospitality, and history, with many staples dating back to ancient times.

Why choose TT'S Lounge

At TT’s Lounge, we pride ourselves on offering an unforgettable dining experience where authentic oriental flavors meet a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to craft traditional dishes bursting with taste, while our cozy lounge setting makes every visit feel special. Whether you’re stopping by for a casual meal, celebrating with friends, or enjoying a quiet dinner, TT’s Lounge is the perfect place to relax, savor, and create lasting memories.
